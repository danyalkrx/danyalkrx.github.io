The Impact of Calcium Leakage on the Heart's Natural Rhythm and Homeostasis

11-2022

A short article explaining the role of calcium on arrhythmia. I focused my research on the role of the ryanodine receptor 2 (RyR2) rs3766871 minor allele. I discussed how rs3766871 increases the risk of arrhythmia and may result in hypercalcemia. In addition to the article, I created an infographic to create a more accessible option.
