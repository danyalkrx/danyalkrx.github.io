Danuvius guggenmosi

09-04-2023

An article discussing the recent findings pertaining to Danuvius guggenmosi. Danuvius guggenmosi was a primate species found in Germany which was likely to have been bipedal and my research provided an update to a 2019 review written by Dr. Lauren Schroeder. I concluded that the research surrounding Danuvius guggenmosi has provided evidence for bipedality and has many interesting findings, but the research is still too new to definitively conclude if it is the oldest bipedal primate.
